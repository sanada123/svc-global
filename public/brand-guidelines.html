BRAND GUIDELINES & IDENTITY STANDARDS

Logo Usage

Primary Logo

The SVC Global logo should be used as the primary brand mark across all applications. It features the letterform "SVC" with the word "GLOBAL" positioned below, accompanied by a subtle diamond accent element in gold.

Light Background

Standard Logo

Minimum size: 160px width

Dark Background

White Variant

For dark backgrounds

Logo Spacing & Clearance

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "S" in "SVC". This ensures the logo maintains its presence and legibility in all contexts.

Minimum Size

The primary logo should never be reproduced smaller than 160px in width. At smaller dimensions, use the symbol-only version (diamond icon).

✓ DO

  • Use approved color combinations
  • Maintain consistent proportions
  • Keep adequate whitespace
  • Use high-resolution files
  • Center logo on dark backgrounds

✗ DON'T

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Add shadows or effects
  • Change colors without approval
  • Rotate or angle the logo
  • Place on insufficient contrast

Brand Voice & Personality

The SVC Global brand speaks with a voice that is exclusive, refined, and deeply rooted in craftsmanship and heritage.

Brand Pillar Description Example
Luxury Premium quality, exclusivity, and refined taste define everything we do. We cater to discerning customers who appreciate the finer things. "Exquisite craftsmanship meets timeless design"
Exclusivity Limited editions, curated collections, and access to items unavailable elsewhere. SVC Global offers what others cannot. "Members-only access to our most coveted pieces"
Craftsmanship Attention to detail, master artisans, and time-honored techniques. Every product tells a story of excellence. "Handcrafted by master artisans in Europe"
Heritage Deep-rooted tradition, legacy brands, and timeless appeal. We connect customers with pieces that endure. "Three generations of Italian furniture mastery"

Tone Guidelines

  • Sophisticated: Use refined language that reflects the luxury positioning
  • Knowledgeable: Share expertise about products and craftsmanship
  • Welcoming: Make exclusivity feel accessible, not arrogant
  • Authentic: Tell genuine stories about products and artisans
  • Respectful: Honor both English and Arabic speaking audiences equally

Brand Applications

The SVC Global brand identity should remain consistent across all touchpoints while adapting appropriately to different mediums.

Digital Applications

  • Website: Logo in header, consistent typography, gold accents on CTAs
  • Email: Branded header with logo, footer with social links
  • Social Media: Square logo variant, brand color palette, consistent filter treatment
  • Product Images: Clean backgrounds (cream or white), lifestyle context, consistent lighting

Print Applications

  • Business Cards: Logo left side, gold accent line, embossed finish
  • Letterhead: Logo top left, subtle watermark background
  • Brochures: Cover design emphasizes hero image with gold typography overlay
  • Packaging: Minimal design, centered logo, luxury materials

Spacing & Layout

  • Minimum 2rem padding around key content
  • Maximum line length: 65-75 characters for body text
  • Generous whitespace creates premium feel
  • Grid-based layouts with 12-column structure
